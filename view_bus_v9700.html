VOLVO 9700

Flexibility, fuel-efficiency and comfort. The Volvo 9700 is the ultimate choice for a profitable and productive coach business.


With a powerful new engine and new driver’s environment, it will be your driver’s new favourite workplace. And your passengers will enjoy the legendary Volvo comfort and safety. Line-haul or charter, maybe both? The Volvo 9700 is simply ready, for whatever comes ahead.







For any bus operation

The essence of the Volvo 9700 is flexibility. It can be configured to suit any operation. Even allowing you to alter between weekday schedules and weekend charter. It comes with a choice of two equipment levels and four lengths. Add to that three engine specifications, multiple interior options and a range of smart packages, combining features and services in a new way.

Passenger Experience

The Volvo 9700 comes with several comfortable seat versions. Generous, space-efficient and featuring attractive, durable materials. Overhead, there are spacious luggage racks and user-friendly service sets. Perfect climate completes the relaxing interior. And if you specify a wheelchair lift you have two alternative places for installation.
